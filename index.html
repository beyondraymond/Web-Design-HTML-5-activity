Melanie Martinez Returns
with New Music and Movie

Singer-songwriter Melanie Martinez returns after a four-year hiatus with her new album and accompanying film, “K-12,” which was released on Sept. 6. The album highlights many of the struggles we face during our rudimentary years through a feminist lens. Meanwhile, the hour-and-a-half film offers stunning visuals and choreography.

Taylor Swift Prepares to Take Lover on the Road as She Announces 2020 Festival and Stadium Dates


Miss Americana is causing some heartbreak.

Taylor Swift announced her “Lover” tour Tuesday, and the pickings are slim.

The pop star will play just two U.S. venues next summer, two shows at the new SoFi Stadium Los Angeles dubbed Lover Fest West on July 25 and 26, then the corresponding Lover Fest East at Gillette Stadium in Foxborough, Mass., on July 31 and Aug. 1.

“For me, the Lover album is open fields and sunsets and SUMMER,” Swift wrote on Facebook. “I want to celebrate the album and perform it live with you in a way that feels authentic to the music. I want to go to some places I haven’t been, and play festivals for the first time in age…and where we didn’t have festivals, we made some."

Internationally, the “Archer” singer will make stops in Belgium, Berlin, Norway, Denmark, Poland, France, Portugal and Brazil in June and July.

What You Need To Know About SB19, The Pinoy Boy Group The Internet Can't Stop Talking About

SB19, the five-member P-pop group that debuted last year with the single Tilaluha, is back with a freshly-released track titled Go Up. With this, the boys are deadset on breaking new grounds in the local music scene after having spent more than three years of intensive training and preparations under the K-Pop system of Korean entertainment company, ShowBT Philippines Corp.
